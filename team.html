The Diamond Dogs

This isn't a song, but does relate back to The Man Who Sold The World. In the game, the Diamond Dogs are a private military force led by the protagonist, slowing growing in power as the game progresses. In relation to the song, this time by David Bowie and not the cover, Diamond Dogs is an album by David Bowie with aspects based on George Orwell's 1984.
