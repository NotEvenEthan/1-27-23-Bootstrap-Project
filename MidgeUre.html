Midge Ure - The Man Who Sold the World

This website is dedicated to sharing music from the Metal Gear Solid 5: The Phantom Pain soundtrack. They relate to the game's theme and just sound good in general. The Man Who Sold The World covered by Midge Ure is the game's main story-related song as it reveals the twist that the protagonist you're playing is not who you think he is. There is also meaning in choosing the Midge Ure cover rather than using the original song by David Bowie.
